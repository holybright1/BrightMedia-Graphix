About Me

Mr. Holybright Shitiavai

Welcome to my creative space. I'm a passionate graphic designer with a genuine love for visual storytelling and design excellence.

Educational Background:
I completed my BSc in Mathematics and Computer Science at Tom Mboya University, graduating in 2025 with Second Class Upper Division honors. During my second year, I discovered my true passion for graphic design, and this ignited a journey of continuous learning and creative growth.

Professional Journey:
With over 2 years of dedicated experience in graphic design, I've honed my skills to deliver quality designs that tell compelling stories for diverse organizations. My work is driven by the belief that great design communicates more effectively than words alone.

Personal Values:
I am a born-again Christian, and my faith shapes my integrity, creativity, and commitment to excellence in everything I do. I believe in creating designs that are not only visually stunning but also meaningful and purposeful.
